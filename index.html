International Singhania Model United Nations 2019

All About ISMUN

The International Singhania Model United Nations is the largest platform you'll ever get to express your views on rapidly emerging global issues. With an Organizing Committee of over 50 members, excellent infrastructure and global presence, our position as Thane's #1 MUN becomes more undeniable every year - and this year is certainly no exception.

We are delighted to announce the 5th in our annual series of Model United Nations, to give you a unique opportunity to represent yourself and be the voice of a million. Every edition aims to live up to our school's motto - "Offer equal opportunities to all, so that they rise to their fullest potential".

ISMUN 2019 will commence on the 26th of July, and will carry on over a span of 3 days. Will you be a samaritan and save countless lives, or will you overshadow the tyranny of Caligula as you hold your own country's interests above the world's? We can't wait to see what you do.
