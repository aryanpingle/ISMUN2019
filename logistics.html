About The Logistics Team

While plans are laid and postitions assigned, it is the Logistics team that weaves the blueprints into reality. A firm backbone of the ISMUN, its heads and members strive to make every event run smoothly. From the breakfast hall to the committee venues, the Logistics team brings about comfort, efficiency, and ease of communication. Working behind the scenes, they are entrusted with the most difficult part of the event- organisation. They see the ISMUN not through the narrowed view of a delegate or an EB member, which looks only its own committee, but in an all- round, 360 degree view; the big picture whose constituents they have to guide to perfect the result.

Meet the Logistics Co-Ordinators

Ananya Shetty
Logistics Head

Ananya Shetty is everything you need from the Head of Logisitics - extremely energetic, hard working, dedicated and very approachable. She's a trained rifle shooter and also an athlete, so you probably want to make sure you get your work done on time.

She has a bubbly personality and manages to make every situation entertaining, yet ensures that the work is done to her expected level of perfection.

Shreya Agarwal
Navigator

Shreya has a creative mind which helps her to think out of the box. She is a perfectionist but also a procrastinator which may seem almost impossible, but she handles it exceptionally well.

Hardworking and diligent in nature, she will stop at nothing to get the job done. She is a very joyful person until it comes down to her own 'sense of humour'.

Vidhi Tekchandani
Assistant Head

Vidhi Tekchandani is the perfect example of a hardworking person. Calm, composed, and intelligent - and at the same time friendly, enthusiastic and humourous. She is a person who will not only carry out her duties wholeheartedly, but also inspire others to do the same.

With all other amazing people in the logistics team, she too is a team player. With excellent organizing skills, perfection is far below her vision.

Joann Anto
Assistant Head

JoAnn Anto, or Jo as her friends call her fondly, isn't an extraordinaire. But she's a pillar of strength for all those around her. She doesn't possess a magic wand, but her presence makes life around her abundantly vivacious. She brings to the ISMUN team the clarity of thought for problem solving with an unassuming attitude, entailing a world of a difference.
