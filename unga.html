What is the UNGA?

Let's be honest, you already know what it is. It's nothing short of the biggest, and one of the most important organs of the United Nations, if not the most. It is also the most versatile; handling situations from more localised, armed conflicts to global concerns.

The Committee Agenda

Deliberation over the presence of armed forces of UN recognized states, in areas not in the territorial jurisdiction of the concerned state - with special focus on areas of armed conflict.

Summary

It’s 2019, the Great Powers of the World are back to their Cold-War style aggression. The Powers, thanks to their great thirst for world dominance and hegemony, have found a way to monopolize: by arming their allies. The armed forces of various nations are stationed at different bases around the world, because of the various bilateral Status Of Forces Agreements (SOFA) between various nations. The mere presence of such forces has given rise to antagonism, and created differences not just among some rival bloc members, but sometimes it has severed the ties between the host and the visitors too.

With all the international geopolitics playing its role in this Global Theatre, are there any hideous villains lurking behind the scenes? Shall the world achieve détente? Or has the 'Curtain' fallen on the hopes of achieving international peace and security? Come find out in the United Nations General Assembly. Quoting the President of the United States, "IT'S GONNA BE HUUUGE!"

Meet the Executive Board

Siddhant Chandan
Director

With a love for puns and physics, it isn’t really surprising that he has a unique sense of humour. Siddhant is a master at wordplay and manages to get his point across in debates easily. He really loves Marvel references and could probably communicate via Brooklyn Nine-Nine gifs and Marvel quotes, but refrains from it for the benefit of the general public. He enjoys basketball and some real high-spirited debates.

Dhvani Bhide
Assistant Director

Dhvani is often regarded as someone who radiates a very happy energy. She has a great sense of humour; with an uncanny grip on sarcasm, who probably won't hold off on roasting you the minute you give her the chance. She's a bold and headstrong individual who stands by her beliefs, but who's always ready to learn and grow. Aside from being a powerful debater and an orator who makes sure she's heard, she's a singer, writer and is unhealthily obsessed with Disney puzzles.

Abhiroop Bhattacharjee
Moderator

Abhiroop can be described as the right mix of sincerity, humour, composure and creativity. In addition to being an articulate writer, meticulous debater and an eloquent orator, he also loves rapping and composing poems in his free time. Apart from having an obsessive interest in sports, national politics, movies and world history, he also loves spending time in the tranquility of nature and harbours the ambition of becoming a research scientist one day. An affable and friendly personality coupled with a calm demeanor, he is sure to catch your eye in the committee.
