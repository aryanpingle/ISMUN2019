What is the AIPPM?

The All India Political Parties Meet is a dynamic and explosive forum for unrestricted political debate, diplomacy and deliberation free from the legislative control and complex procedures of the parliament. It is a venture to emulate political realities by simulating the various levels of policy and governance existing in our nation today.

The Committee Agenda

Reviewing and analysing the economic policies of the NDA government from 2014 to 2019 with special emphasis on monetary and fiscal policies.

Summary

The AIPPM provides the opportunity to delve into issues regarding national importance and gives students the opportunity to experience the process of policy making of the country. In the last five years India has undergone several decisions on economic policy which have impacted every household in this country. From Demonetisation to GST to financial inclusion to identity and attack on black money to corporate insolvency these policies have impacted the Indian economy in a huge way.
Delegates would have to discuss the efficacies of these policies so as to determine future policies which would be key to the economy.

Delegates, brace yourself for a roller coaster ride of gripping and exciting debate. The country’s future is in your hands.
