About The Marketing Team

The secret behind ISMUN's fame and reach lies in the Marketing Team - finding sponsors for the event, arranging for stalls, brochures, and efficient deals - they are the public face of the MUN before it takes place, the only team having a comprehensive idea of the external inputs and companies associated up with the event. After five years, it wouldn't be inaccurate to say that ISMUN needs no publicising, but presenting each edition as a fresh, vibrant, and intriguing experience; keeping old sponsors eager to contribute and attracting new sponsors - that is in the Marketing Team's hands. In a word, they decide the image of the event, and manage all the work needed behind that banner.
