About The Press Team

In the end, it is the press that sways the opinions of people - that holds in its print, the key to their hearts. The Press team of ISMUN 2019 hopes to do this and more - to commemorate the highlights of every committee, every day, and to provide predictions for the next, obtained from the razor - sharp eyes of our journalists. Through discreet observation and subtle questioning, the journalists perhaps know more about the committee's position than any delegate - and this knowledge pours into their article for the session. The team hopes to present to you three spectacular newsletters - one for each day - that will refresh all the day's happenings for you, and provide a source of sweet memories once the event ends.

Meet the Press Co-Ordinators

Vibha Ramachandran
Press Head

Vibha is an optimistic soul; a vivacious and bright person who loves to socialize with people. There's never a dull moment with her never-ending smiles and her adorable clumsiness. She is known for making people swoon over her delicious pastries and she wishes to pursue Mass Media in the future.

Ananya Prabhughate
Press Head

Ananya, as the meaning of her name suggests, is unique. She is firm believer in the 3 E's - Enjoy, Explore and Experience. Being a trained classical dancer, she is highly expressive and strives to achieve excellence at all that she does. She believes that the world should be one where there is tranquility, prosperity and happiness.
She also believes in giving back to the society that helps us grow. She intends to make a reputable name in the field of economics to realize her dream of a peaceful world.

Sidharth Kudva
Editor

A passionate writer, advocate of sarcasm and a terrible bookworm, Sidharth Kudva can have a spirited conversation or a debate on anything from gender disparity to PewDiePie vs T-Series. He has a sharp sense of humour and MAY offend you, but he earnestly requests all his associates not to take anything too seriously. Having an enthusiastic, energetic and colourful personality, he tends to be loud and outspoken evident from his criminal record of being thrown out of libraries.

Reya Nair
Assistant Editor

Known throughout the ISC section for her puns, Reya has a jovial and nonchalant personality. Apart from being the Hitler among grammar Nazis, she is also an oldie but a goldie, so hum a song from ABBA and get ready to hear the backing track. Jocular and fun to talk to, one can easily befriend her.
