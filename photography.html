About Our Photographers

Seeing is believing, so they say- and our Photography team is here to ensure that you will be able to believe, relive, and (in some cases) beautify your ISMUN experience. Creating memories of the event; capturing evidence of every faux pas, crisis, and diplomatic takedown during committee debates; and immortalising every alliance forged and friendship sworn- this is the aim of this team. So dress your best for this ISMUN, and we assure you that you will certainly not go undocumented. Every speech you feel proud of, every lobbying session you walk away satisfied from- we will make sure you will never forget it.
