The Design Team

Rohit Paranjape
Graphic Designer

If Sheldon Cooper and Monica Geller had a child, it would be Rohit Paranjape. A person that redefines OCD and an aspirant of the visual arts, he has a keen eye for creativity and finds inspration from the most ordinary sources. You could hang out with him in a vintage cafe, a dysfunctional pub or a popular dosa joint - as long as there's food on the table and talk of art, he's fine.

Kshitij Sawant
Multimedia Manager

One of the most reliable and punctual members of the Organizing Committee, Kshitij has (quite literally) worked day and night to edit every single photo on this website. Though he may seem introverted at times, he is as comfortable in the lime light as he is while binge watching entire series of anime. He has - and will - learn the nuances of complex software just to add the finishing touches to videos, photos and anything media-related.
